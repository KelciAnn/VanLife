Sedona, AZ

Sedona is known for its Redrocks mountains. It also has specialty shops of artisan goods. I suggest this as a must stop to any adventure you are looking for.

The town of Payson is so quaint. Everyone is friendly and sweet. Out of Payson you have the National Forests with trails, fish hatchery, winter sleding and so may other options to explore.

The Black Hills National Forest is awesome in the fall season. All the leaves in the different hues of reds and oranges are breath taking.
